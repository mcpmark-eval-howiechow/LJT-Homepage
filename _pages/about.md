I am a first-year PhD candidate at the HKUST NLP Group, supervised by Professor Junxian He. My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning, with particular interests in LLM Reasoning, Reinforcement Learning, Hallucination in Vision-Language Models, LLM truthfulness, and Interpretability.
